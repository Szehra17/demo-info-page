Research Study about New York

New York City, often referred to as "The Big Apple," is a vibrant and dynamic metropolis known for its iconic skyline, diverse culture, and global influence. As one of the world’s leading cultural, financial, and media centers, the city is home to landmarks like the Statue of Liberty, Times Square, and Central Park. With its bustling streets, world-class museums, and endless opportunities, New York offers something for everyone, from aspiring artists to ambitious entrepreneurs.
